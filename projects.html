Malaria Detection

Classification of RBC images using DeepNet (September 2019)

Here is another proof to show power of Deep Learning. Malaria is a mosquito borne blood disease caused by Plasmodium parasites. It's conventional diagnosis process is very tiresome and time consuming. Therefore I constructed a deep learning model for classifying RBC's images into two classes: Infected and not Infected. It quickly predicts the result with more than 96% accuracy.

It is trained on Convolutional Neural Network Architecture. I made this project in following four steps:
1. Image Acquisition and Preprocessing
2. Data Augmentation
3. Creating and Training CNN Deep Neural Net Architecture
4. Prediction and Result
